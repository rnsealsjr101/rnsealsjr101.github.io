Ronald A Seals jr has been doing graphic design for 11 years.He  went to temple University for 4 years to receive his Bachelor degree.3 years after he graduated, he entered in several exhibition.One of them were the 2013 Tyler School of Art Market exhibition.In these exhibition,He displayed his most prominent artwork called Photomontage.It is a technique that allowed him to overlay photos that he have taken with his camera and combine them together like a puzzle.With these designs, they became one of his best work.He sold almost 12 pieces.ANother was the 2108 Temple University Foxtoberfest where displayed his Photomontage and his new work called 2d silhouette.To see more,Check out My Portfolio.
